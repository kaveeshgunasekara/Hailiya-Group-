Our Story

A Century of Rope Innovation

Founded in 1923 as Xiecheng Lace Factory in Qingdao, China, Hailiya Group has grown from a small textile workshop into one of the world's leading manufacturers of high-performance synthetic ropes.

Today, our Australian operations bring this century of expertise directly to Asia-Pacific markets — supplying world-class UHMWPE, Aramid, Vectran and Polyester ropes to marine, defence, safety and outdoor sectors.

Every rope we manufacture meets the most stringent international standards — CE, EN892, EN1891, UIAA — with an in-house R&D team and patented technologies developed alongside Tsinghua University.

Notable Projects

Trusted by the World's Greatest Missions

For decades, Hailiya Group has provided ropes and technical support to the most demanding frontier programs — from deep-sea exploration to space launches.

🤿
Jiaolong Deep-Sea Diving Vehicle
Supplied critical rope systems for China's "Jiaolong" manned deep-sea submersible — the nation's most celebrated deep-sea exploration platform, diving to over 7,000 meters.

🚀
China Space Program
Provided rope and cable systems for China's manned spacecraft program, demonstrating performance at the extreme end of safety, weight, and reliability requirements.
🔴
Mars Landing Plan
Technical support for China's interplanetary Mars landing mission — requiring rope solutions certified for unprecedented environmental extremes and zero failure tolerance.
🧊
Polar Scientific Research
Equipment and technical support for Chinese Antarctic and Arctic polar expeditions where temperatures plunge below −40°C and conditions are among the harshest on Earth.
⚓
Navy Ship Anchor & Operation Systems
Engineered mooring and anchoring rope systems for the Chinese Navy, combining anti-rebound technology and extreme load capacity for surface vessel operations.
🌊
National Ocean Strategy Program
Long-term partner to China's national ocean strategic leading project, supplying specialized marine ropes to support China's expanding ocean economy and research capabilities.
