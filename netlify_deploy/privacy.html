Privacy Policy

How Hailiya Group Australia collects, uses, and protects your personal information

Last updated: May 2026. This Privacy Policy applies to Hailiya Group Australia and is governed by the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

1. About Us

Hailiya Group Australia is the exclusive Australian distributor for Qingdao Huakai Ocean Science & Technology Co., Ltd. — a manufacturer of world-class high-performance rope systems with over 100 years of history. We supply marine, defence, safety, and outdoor rope technology to professional buyers across Australia and the Asia-Pacific region.

In this Privacy Policy, "we", "us", and "our" refer to Hailiya Group Australia. Our contact details are set out in Section 7.

2. Personal Information We Collect

We collect personal information only when you voluntarily provide it to us — primarily through the contact and enquiry form on our website. The information we may collect includes:

  • Full name — to address you properly in correspondence
  • Company or organisation name — to understand your business context
  • Email address — to send you our response and any product information you request
  • Phone number — to follow up your enquiry if required
  • Message content — the details of your rope or product enquiry

We do not collect sensitive information (as defined in the Privacy Act 1988), financial information, or government-issued identifiers through our website.

When you visit our website, our web server may also record standard access logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited, and referral URL) for security and performance monitoring purposes. This information is not linked to your personal identity unless required for security incident investigation.

3. How We Use Your Personal Information

We use the personal information you provide for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your product enquiry or request for a quote
  • To provide you with relevant product information, specifications, and pricing
  • To prepare and deliver a quotation or product proposal
  • To arrange samples, technical consultations, or order fulfilment where applicable
  • To maintain records of our customer communications for quality assurance

We will not use your personal information for direct marketing without your explicit consent. We will not send unsolicited commercial electronic messages under the Spam Act 2003 (Cth).

4. Disclosure of Personal Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

In certain circumstances, we may share your personal information with the following parties, strictly for the purposes described in Section 3:

  • Qingdao Huakai Ocean Science & Technology Co., Ltd. (China) — our parent manufacturer. Where your enquiry requires technical specifications, custom manufacturing, or product availability confirmation that must be sourced from the factory, we may share your name, company name, email, and enquiry details with Huakai solely to enable them to assist in fulfilling your request. Huakai is located in the People's Republic of China, a country that does not have an equivalent level of privacy protection to Australia. By submitting an enquiry to us, you acknowledge that your information may be disclosed to this overseas recipient for this purpose.
  • Service providers — we may use third-party service providers (such as email hosting or website infrastructure) to operate our business. These providers are engaged under confidentiality obligations and are not permitted to use your information for their own purposes.

We will not otherwise disclose your personal information without your consent, unless required or authorised by Australian law (including for law enforcement purposes).

5. Storage and Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the personal information we hold from misuse, interference, loss, unauthorised access, modification, and disclosure. These steps include:

  • Secure transmission of data via HTTPS (TLS encryption) on our website
  • Restricted access to personal information within our organisation, on a need-to-know basis
  • Use of reputable, industry-standard email and storage systems
  • Periodic review of our information security practices

While we take these precautions, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of information transmitted to us over the internet.

We will retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by law. When your personal information is no longer needed, we will take reasonable steps to destroy or de-identify it.

6. Your Rights Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988

Under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles, you have the following rights in relation to your personal information:

  • Right of access — You may request access to the personal information we hold about you. We will respond to your request within a reasonable time and, in most cases, within 30 days.
  • Right to correction — If you believe personal information we hold about you is inaccurate, out of date, incomplete, irrelevant, or misleading, you may ask us to correct it. We will take reasonable steps to correct the information or, if we disagree, provide you with a written explanation.
  • Right to make a complaint — You have the right to make a complaint if you believe we have mishandled your personal information or breached the Australian Privacy Principles. Please contact us first using the details in Section 7. If you are not satisfied with our response, you may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at www.oaic.gov.au or by calling 1300 363 992.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 7. We may ask you to verify your identity before processing your request.

We will not charge a fee for making an access or correction request, but may charge a reasonable fee to cover the cost of providing access if the request is complex or time-consuming.

Your continued use of our website or submission of enquiries after any changes to this policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
